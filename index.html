About Me

Born and raised in Roswell, Georgia, I traveled only a few dozen miles south to Atlanta when I decided to pursue my college education at the Georgia Institute of Technology. In the Spring of 2025, I graduated with Highest Honors and obtained my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science. The program I studied in focused on the fields of Media and Intelligence (AI), but my curiosity has led me to dip my toes into several other fields of CS, including Cloud Computing, Data Science, and Computational Theory.

In August 2025, I started my current job as a Software Engineer for

  • Lead Out Software
    • , a growing company located in my hometown of Roswell. I have taken on a critical role in LO's effort to expand our lottery gaming platform to new providers and operators. The job has given me a lot of valuable experience with the end-to-end development of web-based applications. Specifically, I've been integrating our gaming platform with various gaming operator's wallet APIs, which allows us to provide (AKA sell) the games we own to them. Additionally, I've been involved in reverse integrations, where I rearchitected an internal backend service to a secure, externally-exposed API that outside providers can use to add their games to our phyiscal gaming terminals. Moreso than writing the actual code itself, my duties span the entire CI/CD pipeline. We leverage Google Cloud to maintain scalable, serverless applications, so I split my time between debugging, testing, building, and deploying applications - although most of it is spent debugging ;(
